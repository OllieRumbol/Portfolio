Software engineering is now a part of many companies, not just tech companies. Companies such as finance, insurance, logistics, retail, and so much more. Thus meaning its more common to see companies having software engineering departments either for internal tools or as an actually product.

However regardless of what the company does one shared problem that can occur is the idea of technical debt. In this article I want to have a more open and honest conversation about it. What it is, how it affects companies, impact it has, when is it a problem, what to do about, etc.
